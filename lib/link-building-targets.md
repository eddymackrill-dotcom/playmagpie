# Link-building target list — playmagpie.com

Concrete, named, by-hand targets. "Grey hat in effort, white hat in method" — push hard on
volume of genuine outreach; never cross into paid/manipulated links (gambling gets
disproportionate manual review). Grounded in `lib/backlink-recon-2026-06-09.md` (which proved
there's no editorial shortcut to copy — competitors are PBN-built; the durable links are
earned via journalist sourcing, guest contributions, and an original-research asset).

**The one penalty line that governs everything:** if money or reciprocity changes hands for a
link and it isn't tagged `rel="sponsored"`, it's a link scheme. Editorial links earned on merit
(a journalist quotes you, an editor accepts your genuine article, a writer cites your data) are
the goal. Everything below states its own line.

**Where the dofollow value actually is:** journalist sourcing (§5), genuine guest contributions
(§3), and skyscraper citations (§4) — the editorial routes. Directories, forums and content
platforms (§1, §2) are almost all **nofollow** — real value is referral traffic + brand + reach,
not ranking juice. Spend effort accordingly.

## 4. Skyscraper / mention outreach (the asset's whole purpose)

The Transparency Report is better original research than competitors' thin listicles — that's the
hook. Target the kind of site that already links to weaker competitor resources and would plausibly
cite stronger, sourced data.

- **Find the targets:** while the DataForSEO trial is live (cancel 2026-06-22), pull `backlinks`
  on a specific competitor *resource page* (e.g. a cryptomaniaks/cryptocasinos "bonus terms" or
  "withdrawal" article) to list the blogs/news that cited it. Or Google for pages discussing
  "crypto casino bonus terms / wagering / withdrawal limits" that link out.
- **Target type:** mid-tier crypto blogs, personal-finance/gambling explainers, and "is X casino
  legit" writeups — sites that cite *data*, not the mega-publishers (their links are PR-driven,
  unreachable).
- **Approach (email the author):** "You linked to [competitor's bonus rundown] — we just published
  bonus/withdrawal terms verified against each operator's *live* T&C (the gap between headline and
  reality), every figure sourced. Might be a stronger citation for your readers: [report URL]."
- **Effort:** Med–High, rejection-heavy. **Follow:** dofollow when earned.
- **Penalty line:** genuine "here's a better source" outreach only — no payment, no "link to me and
  I'll link to you." Editorial merit is the only currency.

single best dofollow-link-to-effort ratio available and carries zero penalty risk** — start here.
